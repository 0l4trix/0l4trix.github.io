Our team

3-sided Dice Games is an independent video games studio located in Budapest and founded in 2024. Our aim is to make our dream worlds real at least in the virtual space and share them with others. We're a small company only just starting out, with few games, but we're working on changing that. The signature of our games are (bad) humour, sarcasm and old-school pixel art. Oh, and heaps of bad ends!

  • Me - aka One: The dreamer. Thinks out projects then gets Two to make them into reality. Responsible for the design and most of the writing. Found any misspellings or bad grammar? It's their fault.
  • Myself - aka Two: Our programmer. Does 90% of the work. Makes One's ideas into reality. Still new to coding though, so don't go hard on them if you find any bugs.
  • I - aka Three: Mascot of the team. Keeps up morale. Also, the one to blame for any and every bad pun found in our work.
