Cyverse

Navigate the gritty underworld of a neon-lit dystopian metropolis in this immersive point-and-click adventure game. As a skilled hacker on the run from a corrupt government, you must uncover dark secrets, solve intricate puzzles, and make morally ambiguous choices that will shape the fate of the city.

With stunning pixel art visuals, a pulsating synthwave soundtrack, and a gripping narrative filled with twists and turns, this cyberpunk point-and-click game will challenge your wits and keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end. Dive into the shadows and unravel the mysteries of this cybernetic world. Do you have what it takes to survive or will you die trying and possibly doom the city as well?
